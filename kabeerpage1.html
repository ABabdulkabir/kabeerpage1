    (2) Abdullah bin masood ne Rasoollallah Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam se pucha kaunsa kaam ALLAH ko sabse ziyada pasand hai, Aap Sallallahu Alaihi wasallam ne farmaya waqt par namaz Padhna

    (4) Namaz ko qaym karne per bhut bade bade wade or namaz ko chhodne per sakht se sakht wa eeden warid hui hen
    (5) Hazrat Abu Hurairah se riwayat he, rasool sallallahu alayhi wasallam ne irshad farmaya: qayamat ke din Aadmi ke aamal me sabse pehle farz namaz ka hisab liya jayga. Agar namaz durust hui to wo kamyab hoga or agar namaz durust na hui to wo nakam or khasare(nuqsan)me hoga.( sunane tirmizi :413, sunane nisayi: 465, sunane ibne maja: 1425)
